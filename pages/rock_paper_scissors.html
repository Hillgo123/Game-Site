Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game usually played between two people, although in this game you play against a bot. The game has only three possible outcomes other than a tie: a player who decides to play rock will beat another player who has chosen scissors ("rock crushes scissors"), a play of paper will beat a play of rock ("paper covers rock"), and a play of scissors will beat a play of paper ("scissors cut paper").

Rules:

  • Each player chooses one of the three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  • Players reveal their choices simultaneously.
  • The winner is determined by the rules mentioned above:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    • Scissors beat Paper
    • Paper beats Rock
  • If both players choose the same option, the game results in a tie.

About

Welcome to our Mini Game Site! This platform was created as a school project by Leo Bergqvist. My aim is to provide a collection of entertaining and classic games that can be enjoyed by users of all ages. The games featured in our collection include Rock, Paper, Scissors, Sudoku, and Four in a Row. Each game has been carefully developed to ensure smooth and user-friendly gameplay. Feel free to explore and enjoy these timeless games, and remember: the most important thing is to have fun!
